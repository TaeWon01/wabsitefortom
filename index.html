Introduction

Saipan is the largest island of the Northern Mariana Islands, a commonwealth of the United States in the western Pacific Ocean. The western side of the island is lined with sandy beaches and an offshore coral reef creates a large lagoon. The eastern shore is composed primarily of rugged rocky cliffs and a reef. According to 2017 estimates, Saipan's population was 47,565.

The island of Saipan has a total of 30 "official" villages. As a google map of Saipan, there are 8 big villages such as Garapan, Susupe, Capital Hill, etc. However, there are many sub-areas and neighborhoods located in certain villages.
